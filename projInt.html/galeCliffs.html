The Gale Cliffs

The Gale Cliffs are a region dominated by massive stone cliffs rising above the ocean. Its steep walls hide the ruins of cities and villages, destroyed after being abandoned by most of the inhabitants.

History

Long before the events of the game, the Gale Cliffs prospered thanks to whaling, where locals transported whale carcasses to other regions of the archipelago, ensuring economic growth and development.

However, prosperity did not last forever. Over time, whales slowly disappeared until none were seen at all. This collapse in trade destroyed the local economy and led to mass emigration.

20 years later, the player meets a humble village where a retired whaler lives, who tells us about the old glory days of Ingfell. His brother lives in another ruined village destroyed by a sea beast.

In the ruins, the player meets a Hermit, who explains he fled after a fight with his brother, taking a family crest with him. He wants to return to Ingfell but must repair their relationship first.

After finding the family crest to the south, the player also encounters the greatest local danger: a serpent hidden among the rock formations, ready to strike. The Hermit is reunited with his brother, the retired whaler, who is moved and gives the player explosives to continue the story.

Points of Interest

  1. Traveling Trader and Trading Post

    The Traveling Trader provides access to the Dusty Pontoon, located north of N4, with access to:

    • Storage: allows access to personal storage
    • Fish Market: sell fish and buy crab pots
    • Floating Dock: upgrade the boat using dredging equipment resources
    • Shipyard: repair the boat and buy items such as rods, engines, nets, lights, and more
    • Recording Rarities: offers missions to hunt rare fish

  2. Ingfell

    • Retired Whaler: after reuniting with his brother, he gives the player explosives and offers more at the whaling port
    • Ingfell Resident: gives the player the “Valid Until” task
    • Serpent: usually appears near the player and begins a chase when the boat enters its range. Deals two damage to the boat if it hits.

  3. Campsite

    Abandoned campsite where the Old Mayor can be found in panic, speaking incoherently, likely traumatized after witnessing the Leviathan kill the player’s wife.
