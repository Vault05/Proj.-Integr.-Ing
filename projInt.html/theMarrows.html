The Marrows

The Marrows are the starting location for the player. It is a temperate, wooded archipelago that serves as the heart of early fishing and navigation activities. The region is home to small isolated communities, ancient secrets, and the first key contacts for the Fisherman’s journey.

History

The journey begins when the Fisherman (the player) travels toward Greater Marrow after responding to a job advertisement. However, dense fog causes him to crash into the sharp rocks of the bay, completely destroying his boat.

The Mayor rescues him and conveniently provides an old, rusted vessel so he can work. In exchange, the player takes on a debt to the town, paid by giving a percentage of fishing income to the Fishmonger.

As days pass and the Fisherman ventures out at night, he begins to notice disturbing secrets beneath the sea. The turning point occurs when he catches his first Aberration — a grotesque, mutated fish.

When brought to the Fishmonger, the creature contains a mysterious item in its stomach: an Elegant Handkerchief, the first of five ancient Relics.

A mysterious man later directs the player to Blackstone Isle, where the Collector reveals his obsession with relics and offers dredging equipment in exchange for them.

From this moment, the main quest is set: explore the archipelago and recover all remaining relics.

Points of Interest

  1. Greater Marrow

    The main town and starting port for the player after the shipwreck.

    • Mayor: First character the player meets, who manages the island.
    • Fishmonger: Buys fish and aberrations, and sells crab pots.
    • Carpenter: Upgrades and repairs the boat and sells equipment.
    • Lighthouse Keeper: Maintains the lighthouse and watches the fog.

  2. Little Marrow

    The smaller counterpart of Greater Marrow, located across the bay.

    • Merchant: Buys dredged valuables.
    • Painter: Customizes the boat’s appearance.
    • Stevedore: Local resident handling mysterious deliveries.
    • Grieving Father: Searches for his missing son, lost at sea.

  3. Blackstone Isle

    A private and isolated island at the center of the region.

    • The Collector: A mysterious man who trades relics for dredging equipment.

  4. Steel Point

    A small abandoned island in the north of the region.

    • Development: May be developed into a house depending on player choices.
