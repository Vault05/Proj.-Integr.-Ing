    Stellar Basin

    Stellar Basin was described as a popular tourist attraction due to its pleasant tropical climate and beautiful waters and skies. With islands surrounding a massive ocean crater, the area is known for its deep waters, the intense presence of bioluminescent organisms that light up the darkness of the sea, and the possibility that something larger may be hiding in the unfathomable depths of this remote place.

    History

    Over the years, the Researcher established herself in a small research base near the region, responsible for much of the current knowledge about the bioluminescent organisms living in the crater.

    However, after the installation of a loud device in the Research Center, a massive tentacle emerged from the crater and began destroying the facility in anger. The Researcher escaped in time and relocated to the Old Fortress, continuing her studies of local fauna.

    After the player arrives and speaks with her, she requests help: the player must bring her a Glowing Octopus, Firefly Squid, and Aurora Jellyfish. After collecting these specimens, she then asks the player to return to the Research Center and retrieve pieces of a prototype.

    With the prototype recovered, the Researcher again asks for help gathering deep-sea samples and gives the player a Repulsion Machine that must be installed in the Research Center to temporarily repel the monster, opening an opportunity to explore the crater and continue the story.

    Points of Interest

    1. Traveling Trader and Trading Post

      The Traveling Trader provides access to the Starlight Pontoon, located west of the center of E2, with access to:

      • Storage: allows use of personal storage
      • Fish Market: sell fish and buy crab pots
      • Floating Dock: upgrade the boat using dredging equipment resources
      • Shipyard: repair the boat and buy items such as rods, engines, nets, lights, and more
      • Recording Rarities: offers missions to hunt rare fish

    2. Research Points

      There are two research points in Stellar Basin used by the Researcher:

      • Research Center: located far southwest at G4, where the Researcher originally settled until being attacked by the crater monster. The Repulsion Machine can temporarily drive it away.
      • Old Fortress: located in the center of D5, where the Researcher took refuge after the attack on the previous base.
