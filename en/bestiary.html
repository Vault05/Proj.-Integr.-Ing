Threat Encyclopedia

The definitive record of all terrors that dwell in the waters and the mind



Serpent

Gale Cliffs

A blind crustacean of biblical proportions that crawls through the rocky channels of Storm Cliff. It destroys rocks and hulls with equal ease when enraged.

Hadal Creature

Stellar Basin

A colossal sea monster whose bioluminescent tentacles block the center of the Basin. It ruthlessly attacks any boat, unless temporarily repelled by laboratory technology.

Mind Suckers

Twisted Strand

Gigantic parasites disguised among the mangrove roots. They hunt by the sound of the engine and, upon surrounding the boat, directly attack the fisherman's sanity.

Hungry Swarm

Devil's Spine

Small, reddish parasitic fish. They do not damage the boat directly, but latch onto the hull, emitting a scalding heat and shriek-like squeals that attract larger predators.

Mother (Eyeless Beast)

Devil's Spine

A gigantic abomination of stone and lava that patrols the submerged ruins. It is relentless and fiercely attacks the player as soon as the Blind School betrays their position.

Mostrous Narwhal

The Pale Reach

An albino leviathan that cracks the glacial ice with its massive horn. To safely cross its territory, the fisherman must distract it by feeding it fresh fish.

Ocular Ooze

The Iron Rig

A floating, gelatinous entity born from the dark liquid spilled from the deep trench. Its tendrils contaminate the water and attack vessels that pierce the black mist.

Leviathan

The Map's Edges

The ultimate creature that encircles the world. Those fools who try to flee the archipelago by sailing into the open ocean are swallowed whole by its colossal jaws.

False Fisherman Ship

Open Ocean | Midnight

A silhouette in the fog with lights identical to those of an allied ship. As you draw closer, the illusion shatters, revealing the teeth of a giant Anglerfish that uses the light as bait.

Birds

Any Region | Medium Panic

Ghostly birds that appear when the captain's mind begins to unravel. They encircle the deck and steal fish and cargo directly from the hold.

Ghost Ship

Open Ocean | High Panic

A swift, spectral vessel that charges directly toward the player on a collision course. Though it appears real, it dissipates into smoke right upon impact, leaving nothing but terror.

JellyBomb and Watersprout

Stellar Basin/Any Place | Night

Crimson tornadoes and floating jellyfish that appear out of nowhere to pursue the vessel. Crossing their path fries the boat's engines and jams equipment.
