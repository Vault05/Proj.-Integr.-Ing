Devil's Spine

It is a volcanic region with several scattered geysers and steep, treacherous mountains surrounded by deep waters. Its reddish landscape is made up of smoke columns visible in the distance and ruins of a forgotten civilization. Fragments of ancient structures can be found across the islands, suggesting that Devil's Spine was once an important religious or ceremonial center.

History

While navigating the ruins in the region, the player will encounter the Ancient Temple, mere remains of something once worshipped. Three hooded statues surround it, holding empty stone crucibles. Further back, carved pillars show symbols describing hostile creatures and how to avoid them. The Ancient Temple is also home to the Fanatic, who appears to have gone mad.

When interacting with the Fanatic, he calls the player the Herald of Purge, sending them to the three Deep Shrines to collect three Immeasurable Flames for the statues and begin the "Purification Ritual". Each shrine requires fish sacrifices.

After collecting the flames, they must be placed into the stone crucibles. The Fanatic begins the ritual, chanting in an unknown language. A blue flame descends from the sky and he enters it, burning until a bright light obscures everything, leaving only his clothes on a burned circle beside a clock that continues the story.

Ancient Ruins

  • The Cave

    The cave is poorly lit, with paintings on both walls. The left wall shows the birth of a civilization protected by a great lighthouse repelling a tentacled sea creature.

    The right wall shows the fall of the civilization, where a volcanic eruption destroys the lighthouse and monsters devour people as buildings collapse into ruins.

    This appears to be the history of the civilization that once lived in Devil's Spine.

  • Ancient Lighthouse

    The Ancient Lighthouse is a ruined stone structure and one of the tallest remaining buildings in Devil's Spine.

    It is initially locked and requires the Stone Plates quest and the Molten Plate to enter. Inside, the stairs have collapsed. The player is rewarded with the Sky Flame lantern, two chalices, a Citrine Ring, and a Ruby Ring.

    After collecting everything, future visits only show the ruined structure.

Points of Interest

  1. Traveling Trader and Trading Post

    The Traveling Trader provides access to the Burnt Pontoon, located west of the center of E2, with access to:

    • Storage: allows use of personal storage
    • Fish Market: sell fish and buy traps
    • Floating Dock: upgrade the boat using dredging equipment resources
    • Shipyard: repair the boat and buy items like rods, engines, nets, lights, and more
    • Recording Rarities: missions to catch rare fish

  2. Flames of the Deep

    Activities given by the Fanatic to start the "Purification Ritual":

    • Ancient Temple: located far southeast at N13, where the Fanatic, statues, and pillars are found
    • Deep Shrines: located in multiple areas requiring Pale Skate, Ghost Shark, Squid Lobster, Spider Crab, and aberrations
